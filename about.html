Background

My name is Devon St.Clair Harless. I am 'webbie' living and working in Portland, Oregon.

I’m a 50/50 sort of person. Give me design. Give me development. I fuse different interactive disciplines in my efforts to explore the capabilities of the web.

I firmly believe that web designers have the power and responsibility to make browsing an easier and more memorable experience for everyone.

Standards-compliancy, semantics, object-oriented programming, intuitive design—these aren’t just token phrases, but the future of the Web.

Stay curious - we’ve only seen the tip of the iceberg.

Work & Skills

I've received my Bachelors of Science from The Art Institute of Portland in Web Design and Interactive Media. I specialize in front-end design and development—ranging from OOP Flash development to standards-compliant coding.
